Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is made with rice, some type of meat and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables or paneer for the meat. Sometimes eggs and/or potatoes are also added. Source: Wikipedia

This particular recipe will explain how to make a delicious Hyderabadi Chicken Dum Biryani at home. Happy cooking!
Tip!: It tastes better with beer.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 kg basmati rice
  • 1/2 kg chicken
  • biryani masala (you can buy it from any indian store)
  • 2 large onions
  • ginger & garlic paste
  • a bunch of coriander and mint leaves

Steps

  1. Add biryani masala to the chopped chicken pieces and marinate for about 30 minutes.
  2. Fry chopped onions in oil until golden brown and add the marinated chicken pieces and some chopped mint to it.
  3. In a different pot, open cook the basmati rice until it's partially done. About 70%.
  4. Once the chicken is also partially cooked, add the basmati rice on top of the chicken. Spread the rice evenly on top.
  5. Close the lid of the pot and cook for about 30 minutes on a mild heat.
  6. Once cooked, add some chopped coriander on top and enjoy!
