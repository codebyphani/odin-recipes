Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices. Source: Wikipedia

Every layer of this delicious veggie lasagna has its own flavor profile. And hey, it tastes better with red wine.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound cremini mushrooms
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 1 small red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes
